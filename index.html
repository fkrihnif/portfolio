POS (Point Of Sale) Web App

POS Web App This application consists of 2 main actors, namely admin and cashier. The available features are category names, products, transactions, purchase reports, sales reports, return reports, profit and loss reports, and best selling items. Sales receipt printing and automatic price tag creation along with barcodes are also available. This application is connected to a barcode scanner and thermal printer to facilitate your sales business. created using Laravel, Jquery, Ajax, Bootstrap, and MySQL.
